Description

This ontology defines the core product-related concepts and properties used in the Product Module of a Digital Product Passport (DPP), focusing on the description, classification, and technical characteristics of products.

ManufacturingRole c

IRI http://www.w3id.org/dpp#ManufacturingRole
Description

A specialized role denoting the responsibility or action of producing goods in large quantities, typically by machinery. This role is primarily associated with manufacturers who engage in the production of products or components. The manufacturing role encompasses various processes including assembly, quality control, and packaging, ensuring that products are produced according to specified standards and specifications.
