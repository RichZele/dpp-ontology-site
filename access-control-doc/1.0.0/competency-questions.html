Competency Questions

Digital Product Passport Access Control ontology (dpp-ac) - version 1.0.0

The six competency questions below were used to evaluate the representational adequacy of the ontology. Each is formalized as a SPARQL SELECT query, evaluated over the ontology together with the example policies and the scenario knowledge graph, independently of the rule pipeline. A non-empty and correct answer indicates that the ontology can represent and return the knowledge the question asks for.

CQ1 - Role permissions

Which role is permitted which action on a given DPP resource category, and at what sensitivity level?

competency-questions/cq01.rq

CQ2 - Permission coverage

When no access policy applies to a stakeholder role, can that absence be identified as the structural basis for closed-world default-deny?

competency-questions/cq02.rq

CQ3 - Attribute conditions

Which attribute conditions, such as a valid certification or a required trust assurance level, does an access policy attach to a resource?

competency-questions/cq03.rq

CQ4 - Delegation

Which time-bounded delegations does the ontology represent, with their parties, covered resource and action, and validity window?

competency-questions/cq04.rq

CQ5 - Purpose

Which purposes does a policy permit, and which declared purpose is retained on a request?

competency-questions/cq05.rq

CQ6 - Decision provenance

For each recorded decision, can the ontology return its outcome, timestamp, originating request, and applied policy?
