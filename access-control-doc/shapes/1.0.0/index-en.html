SHACL Shapes Graph — Companion Artifact

Abstract

This is the SHACL shapes graph for the DPP Access Control ontology. It defines node and property shapes co-designed with the dpp-ac core T-Box, operating under the Closed World Assumption. The shapes are kept separate from the OWL ontology to prevent reasoner misinterpretation of SHACL triples.

Note: SHACL shapes are validation constraints, not OWL vocabulary terms. They are therefore documented here as a companion artifact rather than with the WIDOCO vocabulary template used for the dpp-ac core, roles, data-categories and GDPR modules.
