Description

This ontology defines key material-related concepts and properties for the Material Module of a Digital Product Passport (DPP), supporting the semantic representation of material specifications, classifications, and sustainability indicators.

Material c

IRI http://w3id.org/dpp#Material
Description

A substance or mixture of substances used in the construction of a Product or its Component(s), which can have various properties and environmental impacts. Examples of materials used in Products include metals, plastics, glass, and electronic components.
